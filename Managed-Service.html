Managed Service

For large scale hiring we provide an established service of sourcing high calibre candidates for our clients at a fraction of the price of traditional recruitment fees or advertising services.

The only fee payable is the upfront Managed Service cost, there are no further fees when you hire our candidates, giving you total visibility on your recruitment costs vs. budget.

This is a highly effective low cost recruitment option that has allowed many of our clients to fill multiple roles on a budget and to benefit from the services that larger organisations would get from a team of internal recruiters with the added benefit of our clients being able to fill their confidential roles under our brand.

The service we provide

  • Employer branding
  • Advertising roles
  • Reviewing applications
  • Informing candidates that their CV is being submitted to your Company and sending over CVs
  • Your Company can then engage with candidates directly and arrange interviews to fill the roles
